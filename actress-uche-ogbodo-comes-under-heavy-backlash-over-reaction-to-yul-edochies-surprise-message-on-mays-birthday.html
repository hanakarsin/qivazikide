Actress Uche Ogbodo Comes Under Heavy Backlash Over Reaction To Yul Edochie's Surprise Message On Ma

Nollywood actress Uche Ogbodo has come under heavy backlash over her reaction to Yul Edochie’s post on his first wife, May Edochie, birthday

Naija News reports that May took to her Instagram page to thank God for her new age as she turned a year older today, the 16th of September 2022.

She wrote: “DEAR LORD, I’M MOST GRATEFUL FOR AN ADDITIONAL YEAR ALONG WITH YOUR EXTRAVAGANT GRACE, MERCY AND BLESSINGS UPON MY LIFE. YOU EVEN MADE ME A QUEEN. I SAY A VERY BIG THANK YOU LORD.”

Joining his wife to facilitate, Yul took to his Instagram page to celebrate and wish her a wonderful year.

He wrote: “HAPPY BIRTHDAY TO MY WIFE, MY CEO EXTRAORDINAIRE, QUEEN MAY YUL-EDOCHIE @MAYYULEDOCHIE. I WISH YOU MANY MORE BEAUTIFUL AND SUCCESSFUL YEARS. MAY GOD CONTINUE TO GUIDE YOU, PROTECT YOU AND BLESS YOU BEYOND YOUR IMAGINATION. LOVE YOU FOREVER.”

Reacting to Yul’s post, Ogbodo took to the comment section to also wish May a happy birthday.

However, actress Ogbodo’s reaction was not appreciated by netizens who dragged her in the mud for being two-faced.

According to the reactions, the actress supported Yul Edochie’s marital affair with a colleague, Judy Austin.
