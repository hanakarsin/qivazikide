Brandon Spink - Age, Family, Bio

Model and actor who landed roles in the 2016 films Mother's Day, Miracles from Heaven and Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice. He has also had a recurring role on ABC's Once Upon a Time. 

Before Fame

He was seven years old when he began performing on stage in local theater productions.

Trivia

He played on an all-star baseball team in his hometown in Georgia.

Family Life

He has two younger brothers, one being fellow actor Roman Spink.

Associated With

He played a Young Bruce Wayne to Ben Affleck's older Bruce Wayne in Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice.
