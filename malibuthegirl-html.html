Malibuthegirl - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok creator known as malibuthegirl, who has earned more than 20,000 fans with her spirited lip sync videos, which are often shot outdoors and feature high-energy dancing. She has also been known to upload vlogs to her YouTube channel and post pictures to her VSCO account. 

Before Fame

She grew up in Idaho and has played sand volleyball.

Trivia

She became an official ROCK Squad Member and promoter for Rock Your Hair Products in 2017. She has a secondary TikTok account as maliburobbinson.

Family Life

She has an older brother named Boston, who sometimes appears in her videos. 

Associated With

She has lip synced to songs like Cash Cash and Bebe Rexha's "Take Me Home."
