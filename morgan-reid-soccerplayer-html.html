Morgan Reid - Age, Family, Bio

Soccer player who is known for playing on the Duke team as number 24. She is also known for posting modeling pictures and other athletic shots to her Instagram for her 180,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She posted her first picture to her Instagram on April 9, 2012. 

Trivia

As a player for Duke, she is known for having 17 straight wins and winning the ACC Championship. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. She can be seen photographed with her sister Madison on her Instagram. On February 23rd 2022, she announced her engagement to basketball player Grayson Allen.

Associated With

Other notable alumni from Duke include Belle Knox, Rita Volk, and Dylan Lauren.
