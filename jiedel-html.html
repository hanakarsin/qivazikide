Jiedel - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube gaming personality who has earned massive popularity for his NBA 2K gaming playthroughs, hacks, and montages. He'd later become a member of the NBA 2K focused web group 2Hype.

Before Fame

He began his YouTube channel in June 2014. 

Trivia

He has amassed more than 1.1 million YouTube subscribers to his channel. He has posted many photos and videos of himself playing basketball on his jiedel Instagram account. 

Family Life

His real name is James Riedel. He has dated a girl named Amy.

Associated With

He and his brother JesserTheLazer have collaborated on his YouTube channel as well as became members of 2Hype.
