Amy King - Age, Family, Bio

Best known as the niece of reality stars Michelle and Jim Bob Duggar from 19 Kids and Counting. She became extremely popular on Instagram, earning over 440,000 followers to her amyrachelleking account. 

Before Fame

She first met her husband Dillon King in 2007, a year before 19 Kids and Counting premiered. 

Trivia

She gained national attention when her muddy engagement photos were featured on websites like People and Us Weekly.

Family Life

She was born in Arkansas but moved to Nashville, Tennessee to pursue a career in country music. In 2015 she and Dillon wed. Thier son Daxton was born in 2019. 

Associated With

She is a family member of TLC reality stars like June Shannon.
