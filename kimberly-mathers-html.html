Kim Mathers - Age, Family, Bio

Ex-wife of Rap superstar Eminem, born Marshall Mathers, who first married him in 1999 and divorced in 2001. They would remarry in January of 2006 but divorce 3 months later.

Before Fame

She met Marshall through a mutual friend when she was 13 years old and they dropped out of school together.

Trivia

She was involved with another man in between marriages with Marshall and had a daughter named Whitney during that time.

Family Life

She gave birth to Eminem's famed daughter Hailie on Christmas day of 1995. She has another biological daughter named Stevie, whom Eminem adopted. She and Eminem also adopted her sister Dawn's daughter Alaina. Her twin sister Dawn Scott died of a drug overdose in January of 2016.

Associated With

She was there to congratulate her daughter Hailie Mathers on her homecoming queen victory.
