Lea Elui Ginet - Age, Family, Bio

Instagrammer who has earned over 11 million followers posting pictures of different fashion looks. Several fellow social stars have appeared in her posts such as Loren Gray, Brent Rivera and Hannah Stocking. She also surpassed 16 million fans on TikTok.

Before Fame

She began posting content on musical.ly, which later became TikTok, in August of 2016. 

Trivia

She traveled to San Francisco in August 2018 and she posted a picture from Instagram headquarters alongside Kristen Hancher to her Instagram account. 

Family Life

She is from France and later lived in Los Angeles, California. She dated Hunter Rowland for about a year. She also previously dated fellow social star Lucas Ollinger. She and model Scott Morton have been romantically linked.

Associated With

She did a two-part belly dance contest video on musical.ly where she danced to Shakira and Wyclef Jean's "Hips Don't Lie."
