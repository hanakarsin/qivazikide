Wolfie - Age, Family, Bio

Comedian and rapper who posts comedy videos featuring his parents and friends to his WolfieRaps YouTube channel. He became a member of the social media group CloutGang. 

Before Fame

He created his YouTube channel as WolfieEnt in August of 2013. His first video was a remix of Migos's "Versace." 

Trivia

He reached 7 million YouTube subscribers, additionally, he has more than 1.5 million followers on his wolfie Instagram account. 

Family Life

His real name is Charles Raynor. He is from Ottawa, Canada. He has been dating influencer Sylvia Gani.

Associated With

He has covered artists such as Justin Bieber, Chris Brown, Miley Cyrus and Kid Ink in videos for his YouTube channel.
