Sarah Close - Age, Family, Bio

Singing sensation on YouTube who mainly records covers of top artist's songs. She also wrote a collection of over 300 songs.

Before Fame

She began to learn to play the guitar when she was 14 years old. She started publishing videos on YouTube in January of 2010.

Trivia

She previously worked in promotions for a few months.

Family Life

She is from the Isle of Wight, England and grew up with a brother. She dated YouTuber Callum McGinley, AKA Callux. 

Associated With

She is friends with fellow YouTuber MyNamesChai.
