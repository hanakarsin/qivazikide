Maddie Welborn - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star best known for her self-titled YouTube channel where she posts a variety of vlogs, often related to her family life or daily routine, as well as the occasional reaction video. She has amassed over 250,000 subscribers to the channel. Her Instagram account has featured numerous photos of her children.  

Before Fame

She first became active on social media in early 2013.

Trivia

She gained over 1 million followers on the app musical.ly, which later became TikTok. She was primarily known for her pop lip sync videos on the platform. 

Family Life

Her hometown is Aledo, Texas but she later moved to San Antonio. Her sister Aly appears in her "Messy Sibling Tag" video. She and musician Maxxy Vicious got engaged in June 2018. She has a daughter named Leila Grace Pitt and a son named Braxton Wayne Pitt. She later married Tyler Pitt.

Associated With

She has collaborated on challenge videos with fellow social stars Nick Bean and Zach Clayton.
