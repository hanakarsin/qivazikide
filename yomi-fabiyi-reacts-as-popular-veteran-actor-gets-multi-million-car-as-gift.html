Yomi Fabiyi Reacts As Popular Veteran Actor Gets Multi- Million Car As Gift

Nollywood actor, Yomi Fabiyi has taken to his social media to celebrate his senior colleague, Pariolodo after he received a brand new car.

Recall the actor was gifted a brand new car  after he took to his social media to cry for financial assistance.

Rejoicing with him, Yomi Fabiyi on Friday expressed joy that the veteran wouldn’t be coming to his movie location on bike (Okada).

Describing him as a legend, Yomi Fabiyi prayed for long life in good health for him.

“Baba Pariolodo, astute veteran comedian and brilliant actor. Congrats sir.

“I am grateful to God Almighty, Agbala Gabriel, your fans, and every kind-hearted soul out there. It shall be counted for them for good.

“The Nigerian people through this CHARITY reminded you as a community they never forget your likes, the memory as fresh as ever. That your efforts and sacrifices are never wasted. Your HOPE rekindled.

“I am happy because when I invite you to movie locations again and it won’t be on Okada but in the comfort befitting of a MOVIE STAR and LEGEND. Longer life in good health I wish for you, sir.”
