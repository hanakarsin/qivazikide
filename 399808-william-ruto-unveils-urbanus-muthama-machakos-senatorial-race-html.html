William Ruto unveils Urbanus Muthama for Machakos senatorial race

- The politicians led by UDA chairperson Johnson Muthama resolved to shelve their interest in support of Mutunga

Politician Urbanus Mutunga Muthama will fly the United Democratic Alliance (UDA) flag in the Machakos senatorial by-election after he was endorsed by Deputy President William Ruto.

The endorsement followed intense deliberations spearheaded by UDA chairperson Johnson Muthama which saw other Tanga Tanga-leaning politicians shelve their interest to support Mutunga.

Muthama and grassroots leaders drawn from Machakos constituencies on Thursday, January 14, paid Ruto a courtesy call at his Karen residence to lay the groundwork ahead of the mini poll.

"I'm grateful to witness the partnership forged by the Machakos senate by-election hopefuls in favour of Urbanus Mutunga Muthama Ngengele, a hustler from the county. Our gratitude to them for their audacity and leadership to cede their aspirations and back Ngengele for the race," the DP remarked.

Meanwhile, Wiper Democratic Movement leader Kalonzo Musyoka unveiled Muthama's ex-wife Agnes Kavindu as the party candidate for March 18 senatorial race.

The Machakos Senate seat fell vacant following the death of Boniface Kabaka who was receiving treatment at The Nairobi Hospital.

Kabaka was admitted to the hospital's Intensive Care Unit (ICU) on Friday, December 4, after collapsing at an apartment in Kilimani Estate.

He was pronounced dead by the city-based health facility on the morning of Friday, December 11, after a week of ailing.
