Prezley Lea - Age, Family, Bio

YouTuber and gaming personality who rose to fame by publishing videos about the Adopt Me! Roblox modification. His videos have been viewed more than 587 million times on the platform. They have often featured hacks and insights into the games. 

Before Fame

He published "HOW I TRADED FROM NOTHING TO LEGENDARY!" in January of 2020.

Trivia

His most popular video was viewed more than 2 million times. In the video he shared a secret code for a special animal. Prezley dressed as Beetle Juice for Halloween in 2021. 

Family Life

His family members are also YouTube personalities and he appears on their EMPIRE Family channel. He has a younger sister named Charlotte, also known as Charli and Miss Charli.

Associated With

In September 2019, his family did a challenge called Last to Stop Watching SIS vs BRO.
