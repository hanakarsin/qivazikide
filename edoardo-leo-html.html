Edoardo Leo - Age, Family, Bio

Movie actor and filmmaker who wrote and directed his first film 18 Years Later in 2009. He received a nomination at David di Donatello for Best New Director. He has over 650,000 followers on his edoardoleo Instagram account.

Before Fame

He attended the Sapienza University of Rome where he received a degree in Literature and Philosophy.

Trivia

He made his on-screen debut in the 1995 television movie La Luna Rubata. He then had his breakout with the 2003 TV show Un Medico in Famiglia.

Family Life

He married Laura Marafioti.

Associated With

He and Piero Tosi were both nominated for a David di Donatello award.
