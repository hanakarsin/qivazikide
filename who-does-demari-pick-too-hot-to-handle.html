Demari Fights to Win Back [SPOILER] in Season 6 of 'Too Hot to Handle'

Despite kissing Valentina, Demari wants to move forward with Bri.

Spoiler alert: This article contains spoilers from Season 6 of Too Hot to Handle.

Get ready for Season 6 of Too Hot to Handle to add some sizzle to your life! This latest season brings in a fresh batch of attractive singles, among them Demari Davis, a 27-year-old stockbroker accustomed to catching the eye of single women everywhere.

Sparks fly as he starts connecting with Bri Balram, but things get complicated when Bri kisses Chris Alli, leaving Demari uncertain about their future. Bri quickly apologizes, and they agree to give their relationship a chance.

However, Demari's situation gets more complicated when he goes on a date with newbie Valentina. Now, he's torn between the two lovely ladies. Who will he pick? Keep scrolling for all the known details regarding his decision!

Who does Demari pick on 'Too Hot to Handle'?

After much thought, Demari ultimately decides on Bri. However, it takes her some time to warm up to the idea of being with him again.

Demari tries to reassure Bri that Valentina's presence at the retreat doesn't change his feelings for her, but Bri doesn't want anything to do with him anymore. She says there will be no second chances and adds that Demari can "kiss my a--."

Despite this, they make multiple attempts to resolve their issues. Bri is open to hearing Demari out, though she's uncertain if he can mend their situation until he understands the depth of her hurt.

Demari eventually comes to terms with Bri's reluctance to get to know him better, but he's still pained by what he feels is a genuine connection between them. Nevertheless, he's determined to fight for something he believes could be so special.

In Episode 6, Demari apologizes to Bri again, but she gives him the cold shoulder. Later, while talking by the pool, Demari suggests they share a bed to further their connection, but Bri firmly rejects the idea. She tells him he needs to learn to respect her, emphasizing that he clearly doesn't comprehend the gravity of the situation.

"He's trying to fix it, but it's not fixable," Bri says in a confessional.

He admits he failed to treat her properly and acknowledges that kissing Valentina was a major mistake. Bri senses his sincerity but questions how she can trust him not to repeat his past behavior.

Demari insists that Bri deserves better and is committed to earning her trust. He also expresses his deep respect for her feelings, and Bri agrees to give their relationship another chance.

After pinky-promising that he won't be swayed by anyone else who joins the retreat, Demari and Bri's watches turn green to signal that their kiss is within the rules.
