Webb Simpson - Age, Family, Bio

PGA golfer who surprised audiences by winning the 2012 U.S. Open.

Before Fame

He was named ACC Player of the Year while playing golf for Wake Forest University.

Trivia

He won two PGA events in 2011, including The Deutsche Bank Championship.

Family Life

He married Taylor Dowd in 2012; together, they had two children, James and Willow.

Associated With

He and Tiger Woods represented the U.S. in the 2011 Presidents Cup.
