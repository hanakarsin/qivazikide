Stephen Crichton - Age, Family, Bio

Professional center, fullback, and wing who was signed by the Penrith Panthers of the Australian National Rugby League. Due to his performance, he was named the Centre of the Year during the 2020 Dally M Awards. 

Before Fame

Prior to becoming a professional, he played for the St Clair Comets as an amateur. 

Trivia

He made his professional debut playing against the Cronulla-Sutherland Sharks during the 21st round of the 2019 NRL season. He began playing with the club Canterbury Bulldogs in 2024.

Family Life

His brother Christian is also a professional rugby player. 

Associated With

He played alongside Nathan Cleary as a member of the Panthers during the 2020 NRL season.
