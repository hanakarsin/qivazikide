LonePanthera - Age, Family, Bio

Online gamer known for both her Panthera YouTube channel and LonePanthera Twitch stream. Her channel features vlogs, reaction videos, and Let's Plays of Outlast, MovieStarPlanet, and Until Dawn, amongst other games.

Before Fame

After studying mechanical engineering, she switched her major to mathematics.

Trivia

In addition to her YouTube and Twitch channels, she has also been known to occasionally broadcast on her Panthera YouNow channel.

Family Life

She is of Moroccan and Malaysian descent. Her younger sister is addicted to musical.ly and her videos appear in the popular YouTube videos "REMAKING MY CRINGEY LIL SISTER'S MUSICAL.LYS" and "REACTING TO MY LIL SISTER'S MUSICAL.LY!! so cringey omg."

Associated With
