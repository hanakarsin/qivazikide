BBNaija All Stars: I Was Close To Beating Up Somebody

Big Brother Naija (BBNaija) All Stars housemate, Lucy Edet has opened up on how she was about to beat one of her co-housemates on Monday.

The 33-year-old housemate made this known on Tuesday during a conversation with fellow housemate Pere, stating that she did not want to be disqualified from the show.

Recall that the 33-year-old had requested on Monday to be removed from the show during her diary session with Biggie due to her argument with housemate Kim Oprah.

Lucy, who appeared emotional during her diary session with Biggie, candidly recounted the altercation and clarified that Kim Oprah had misconstrued her intentions during the process.

“Yesterday I was this close to beating somebody up. The last thing I want is to add disqualification to the whole theory people already have of me,” she said.

Lucy joined the other housemates on Sunday alongside Omashola, Prince and KimOprah. All four of them will not be competing for the N120 million grand prize.
