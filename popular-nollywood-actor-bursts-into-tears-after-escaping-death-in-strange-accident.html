Popular Nollywood Actor Bursts Into Tears After Escaping Death In 'Strange' Accident

Popular Yoruba Nollywood actor, Okiki Oshin, burst into tears while expressing appreciation to God after escaping death in a freak accident today.

Naija News reports that Okiki was saved from the strange accident which would have claimed his life along the Lagos-Ibadan expressway.

While thanking God in a video shared online, the actor showed off the vehicle which went into flames along the road.

Amazed at how God saved his life, the actor suddenly broke down in tears.

The actor, who spoke in the Yoruba language expressed appreciation to God for sparing his life as he points to the accident scene, showing how the vehicle continued to burn despite intervention by the fire service.

BBNaija 7: Phyna Beats Other Housemates With New Achievement

A housemate in the Big Brother Naija ‘Level Up’ edition, Ijeoma Josephina Otabor popularly known as Phyna has been verified on Instagram.

The development makes Phyna become the first female housemate to get verified on the social media platform.

The 25-year-old entertainer and self-acclaimed hype priestess of Nigeria have become one of the most talked-about housemates this season.

Phyna who has recently been dejected over her failure to win tasks in the house has 235,000 Instagram followers.

The verification of her Instagram account will excite the supporters of the reality star.
