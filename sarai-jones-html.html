Krazyrayray - Age, Family, Bio

Enormously popular makeup guru whose popular video, "Straight Hair with NO HEAT!," has racked up more than 10 million views. She did a segment on YouTube that compared the beauty routines of guys versus girls. 

Before Fame

She posted her first video to YouTube on July 9, 2009, but didn't start posting videos regularly until late in 2010. 

Trivia

She has worked with the web series network AwesomenessTV. She has more than 3.5 million YouTube subscribers. Her Instagram account, saraijones, has over 900,000 followers and has mostly featured selfies. 

Family Life

Her real name is Sarai Jones. She is from Seattle, Washington and later moved to Los Angeles, California. She began a relationship with Jaeger Scheppe, but the two split in 2015. She then dated Jordon Willis from 2017 to 2018. She began a relationship with Jalen Conway in March 2020. In November 2022, they announced that they were expecting their first child together. They welcomed their son, Crew Seven Conway, on May 30, 2023.

Associated With

She is good friends with fellow makeup guru Amanda Steele. She has featured music by artists such as Taylor Swift and JID.
