Kenyan soldier evokes anger online after firing gun and risking lives on live Facebook video

- A Kenyan soldier risks losing his job after firing a deadly assault gun and risking lives while recording himself on live Facebook video

- The soldier had taken to Facebook to record himself in a Live video while flaunting a Colt AR-15 riffle

- In a surprising turn of events, the soldier accidentally fired one shot while still recording himself

A young Kenyan soldier is likely to lose his job or face dire consequence after firing a deadly assault gun and risking lives on live video.

The soldier whose identity is still a mystery on Thursday, March 8 2018 took to Facebook to record himself in a Live video while flaunting a Colt AR riffle.
