Ben Githae Urges Ladies Not to Turn Down Advances from Broke Men

  • Gospel singer Ben Githae has trashed Benjamin Zulu's sentiments concerning broke people staying away from dating and relationships
  • According to the Tano Tena singer, there are blessed people who married when they had nothing to their name
  • His followers, however, had mixed reactions, with some supporting Zulu's opinion and calling out broke people to put their energy into building themselves first

Gospel singer Ben Githae has advised women not to turn down advances from men who are financially unstable because they may be people who will be blessed in future.

Ben Githae tells ladies to give broke men chance

The Maya Ni Mabataro hitmaker responded to relationship coach Benjamin Zulu's opinion about broke people staying away from relationships until they have money.

This is what Githae posted on his Facebook page:

"I have seen this guy Benjamin Zulu say that broke people should forget about relationships and concentrate on looking for money. Some of the people we consider blessed today, including Barack Obama, married when they were paupers. What if Rachel Ruto despised the DP in the early 90s? What if Mrs SK Macharia looked down upon her husband in the 70s?"
