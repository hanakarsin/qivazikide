Sam Pepper - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube prankster and musician who has used his internet celebrity to perform across the U.S. and U.K. He was a competitor on the 11th season of the Big Brother (UK) and was evicted from his home on the show's 72nd day. 

Before Fame

He was a graffiti artist when he started uploading his first YouTube videos in 2010. 

Trivia

Also a clothing designer, he began working on his own clothing line in August of 2016. 

Family Life

He has a sister and a brother. 

Associated With

He collaborated with Mark Ludford on a Minecraft series in 2011.
