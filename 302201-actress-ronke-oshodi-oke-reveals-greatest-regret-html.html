Actress Ronke Oshodi Reveals Her Greatest Regret

Ondo State born popular Yoruba actress, Ronke Oshodi-Oke, has made her regrets about life known to the general public.

The busty actress, Ronke Ojo-Gbolahan popularly known as Ronke Oshodi-Oke, made it known that she has her regrets in not completing her education to the University level as at when she's supposed to.

The film producer, writer and actress however made it known that no matter how it seems late in getting her university education, she still wants to return to school. She made this known to Encomium magazine in a recent interview, saying:

"My big regret is not going back to school as I had wanted to. I have been planning to return back to school for quite a long time but when you are making money, seeing yourself being celebrated here and there, you will feel on top of the world. But you are nothing without completing your education. So that's the bold step I want to take now. So it's going to be fun. I don't want to agree it's going to be difficult. I really want to go back to school and I mean it this time around".

It would be recalled that just recently, the Nollywood actress and mother was spotted kissing comic actor, Hafiz Oyetoro aka Saka.
