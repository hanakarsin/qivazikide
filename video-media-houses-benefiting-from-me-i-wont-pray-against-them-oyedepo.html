VIDEO: Media Houses Benefiting From Me, I Won't Pray Against Them

President and founder of Living Faith Church Worldwide popularly known as Winners Chapel, Bishop David Oyesepo, had revealed why he does not fight media organisations.

The globally renowned cleric stated that media houses get that contents that keeps them running from his sermon.

Naija News reports that the billionaire preacher stated this during a sermon in his church.

He stated that if he prays against media houses and they are shut down, he would then have to pray for workers of such organisations to get work.

“When I’m doing the things of God, how they feel is irrelevant. I’m privileged to be one of those who sustain the media houses in Nigeria because when they publish what I say, people buy it,” Oyedepo said.

“I can’t fight them because if I fight them and they close down those houses, then I will be praying for them to get a job again.

“I’m having a good time having them market my sayings rightly or wrongly. It keeps a number of media places going.”

Meanwhile, the  wife of Prophet Temitope Balogun Joshua, aka T.B Joshua, Evelyn, have allegedly evicted some disciples of the late prophet from the Synagogue Church of All Nations, SCOAN.

According to sources, the late Prophet TV Joshua’s Wife carried out that eviction as part of moves to assume leadership of the church that has been closed since the burial of the cleric.

“These disciples asked to leave were those who disrespected Evelyn while her husband was alive.

“They sidelined her, It’s a powerful tussle,” the source told Daily Post.
