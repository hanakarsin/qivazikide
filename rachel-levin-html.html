Rachel Levin - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube superstar best known for her channel Rclbeauty101, where she posts video blogs, comedy skits, and beauty/lifestyle videos to her more than 14 million subscribers. 

Before Fame

She was a cheerleader in high school and she began her college studies at Penn State. She published her first video called "How To...Conceal Under Eye Dark Circles" on December 15, 2010. 

Trivia

She published a video called "500K Subscriber Giveaway! [MacBook Pro + iPad + Go Pro]" on August 12, 2014 and followed it with a 600k subscriber giveaway less than a month later. 

Family Life

She was raised in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania with three siblings and two step-siblings. Her sister Daniella has appeared in her videos. She introduced her mom in a YouTube Mom Tag video in 2014. She dated fellow YouTuber Tyler Regan for about two years before the two broke up in late 2019.

Associated With

She created a video called "Birthday Swap with BeautyBySiena" on March 5, 2014.
