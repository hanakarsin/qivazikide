Swifty - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube gaming phenomenon also known as Swiftyirl who is popular on Twitch as well. His main YouTube channel has more than 650,000 subscribers while his swifty Twitch channel has more than 600,000 followers. His swiftywow Instagram account is a personal place separate from his gaming career. 

Before Fame

The first channel he launched was his Swifty channel in November of 2005. He created what would become his main channel under the username johnsju in June of 2006.

Trivia

He partnered with the Twitch stream channel Nvidia. He streams such titles as World of Warcraft, Call of Duty, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Grand Theft Auto, The Witcher, Battlefield, Minecraft, and more. 

Family Life

He filmed alongside his brother Jay and father for a foundation for fatherless kids. He also has a sister named Cathy.

Associated With

He published a video titled "Swifty Legends of the Arena Ziqo vs Sodapoppin."
