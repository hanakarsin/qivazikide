Folasade Yemi-Esan: 7 things you should know about new head of service appointed by Buhari

President Muhammadu Buhari on Wednesday, September 18, appointed Folasade Yemi-Esan as the new acting head of service of the federation.

Yemi-Esan replaced Winifred Ekanem Oyo-Ita who has been directed to go on an indefinite leave to pave way for a clear conclusion of her probe by the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC).

Oyo-Ita is being probed by the anti-graft agency over alleged N3billion fraud in her Office.

Here are five key facts to know about Yemi-Esan, the new acting head of service of the federation.

Education

1. Dr Folashade Yemi-Esan obtained her first degree in Dental Surgery from the University of Ibadan in 1987

Career

2. She served as the Information Attach’e in the Nigeria Information Service Centre, Embassy of Nigeria, Abijdan, Cote d’ Ivoire from 1992 to 1995

3. Yemi-Esan later joined the Federal Ministry of Health where she rose in ranks to become a director

5. From 2012 to 2014, she was the director of information in the State House

7. Before her latest appointment, she was the permanent secretary, Ministry of Petroleum Resources
