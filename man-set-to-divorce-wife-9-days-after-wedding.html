Man Set To Divorce Wife Nine Days After Wedding

A Nigerian who got married to his wife about nine days ago is set to divorce her.

According to a post shared by a Twitter user, @chuddyOzil, the newly married man who tied the knot with his wife on December 27, 2021 caught her cheating on him on January 5, 2022.

He wrote: “Got married (Trad & Church) on the 27th of December 2021, caught with one of her side guy on the 5th of January, locking lips over a bottle of red wine in a cozy environ.

“Husband & umunna already set to embark on the journey to do the traditional rite.

Marriage over! Correction 5th January 2022″

In other news, popular Nigerian comedienne, Adeyela Adebola, professionally known as Lizzy Jay has declared herself as a gold digger.

The comedienne cum actress noted that she has no issue with getting into relationships with people to get money from them.

Lizzy Jay in a post on her Insta stories stated that though she has her own money, she still needs money from anyone who wishes to propose love to her.

She further stated that her money is meant for investments while that of her proposed lover is to be spent lavishly.

Lizzy Jay in the post disclosed that money can buy her love, a slang that is trending on social media.
