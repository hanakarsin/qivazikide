6obby - Age, Family, Bio

Pronounced Bobby, he is a hip hop artist who became known for songs such as "Ghost" and "Nobody." He writes song about his day-to-day life.

Before Fame

He worked at Walmart. He began making music in late 2016. He enjoyed skateboarding in his youth.

Trivia

He got a Tom and Jerry tattoo on his neck. He has earned more than 40,000 followers for his Instagram account.

Family Life

He is from the small town of Graham, Texas. He has been in a relationship with singer BadMoodRude.

Associated With

His music was inspired by a mix of post-hardcore and mainstream rap, specifically groups and artists such as Chief Keef, Asking Alexandria and Bring Me The Horizon.
