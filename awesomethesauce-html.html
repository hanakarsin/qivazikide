AwesomeTheSauce - Age, Family, Bio

Gaming YouTuber known for his channel AwesomeTheSauce. He has gained more than 80,000 subscribers posting Wizard101, Fortnite and Geometry Dash gameplay. 

Before Fame

He created his YouTube channel in July 2014 and debuted in October with the video "Wizard101 Exalted PvP #1: First Vid Sorcerer Swag." 

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos is the December 2016 video "Top Five Unexplained Mysteries in Wizard101." 

Family Life

Born in India, he later lived in Zimbabwe. 

Associated With

Both he and BlazeLifehammer are YouTubers who gained a following for their Wizard101 gameplay.
