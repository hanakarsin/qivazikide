Joe Jitsukawa - Age, Family, Bio

Co-founder, writer, director, and actor for the enormously popular Just Kidding Films team, a YouTube channel that takes on social, contemporary, and cultural issues with a comedic edge. He would make a personal YouTube channel called Joe Jitsukawa featuring life vlogs, cooking videos and rants that earned 460,000 subscribers. 

Before Fame

He studied psychology at Cal State Los Angeles. 

Trivia

He has a personal channel on YouTube as well where he posts vlogs, music, philosophies, random acts of dancing and more. One of his most viewed videos featured him going to Chuck E. Cheese for his birthday in 2011. 

Family Life

He was born in California, but has lived all over the world in Korea, Australia, Texas, Michigan, Japan, China, Taiwan and Thailand. He dated his fellow film partner Jessica Caldwell. 

Associated With

He and his partner Bart Kwan founded JKF while attending the UCLA. Dancer Anthony Lee has starred in skits on Just Kidding Films.
