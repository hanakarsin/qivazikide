Mombasa county single business permit acquittal procedure

It is now easy to apply for and receive a new business permit with the county government of Mombasa. To do this:

  • Visit the ward office for a BR-1 form
  • Fill out the BR-1 form and return it to the ward licensing officer. In response, the officer will inspect your venture in a day bound visit for verification purposes.
  • The inspection done will then serve to gauge payable permit fees. Once determined, the officer will stamp the application and refer you to the main county offices.
  • The central office will approve the application form and then enter detailed information into the Sbps database.
  • Once the business permit is signed, you will be required to fetch it at the dispatch office

Payable fees for Mombasa country single business

Mombasa county single business permit rates are fairly affordable for most entrepreneurs. These affordable rates have attracted massive single businesses in the region making it a sustainable economic hub in Kenya. Below is a breakdown of the payable fees.

Permit Fees

  • Application fees estimated at KSh 200
  • Medium trader level of 5-20 employees are to pay KSh 10,000
  • Large trader level businesses of 21-50 employees pay a fee of KSh 20,000

Validity of single business permit in Mombasa county

Single business permit renewal Mombasa takes place annually. The latter implies that the charges above are payable on an annual basis

Processing Time

The process of processing a business permit in Mombasa Country takes up to three days on average. Depending on the dynamics, the process should be shorter as long as all the required documents are available.
