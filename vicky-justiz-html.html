Vicky Justiz - Age, Family, Bio

Fitness model and YouTube star best known for her self-titled channel where she has gained over 1.8 million subscribers posting exercise tips, guides, and how-to videos.

Before Fame

At eleven, she began training in dance and by the time she got into high school she was playing varsity soccer.

Trivia

In addition to her fitness channel, she has a travel and lifestyle blog called Bottle of Wine for Two.

Family Life

Born in Russia, she moved to the United States in 2001. Her boyfriend Jonathan Urra is also a fellow YouTuber and one half of the Bottle of Wine for Two Instagram account. They are now engaged.

Associated With

In January 2016, she made an appearance on the CBS reality series The Doctors, a show created and produced by Phil McGraw.
