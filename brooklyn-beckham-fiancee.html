Brooklyn Beckham's Fiance, Nicola Peltz, Comes From Major Celeb Royalty Too

On July 11, Brooklyn announced that he and girlfriend Nicola Peltz were engaged. Here's all you need to know about Brooklyn Beckham's fianceé.

They grow up so fast! Brooklyn Beckham, Victoria Beckham’s eldest son with David Beckham, announced that he’s engaged. On July 11, he posted the news to Instagram, writing, “Two weeks ago I asked my soulmate to marry me and she said yes xx I am the luckiest man in the world. I promise to be the best husband and the best daddy one day. I love you baby xx.”
