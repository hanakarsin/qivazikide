Squid Games Yeong-su, Others Win Big At 2022 Golden Globe Awards

Squid Game’s actor, O Yeong-su, and Hollywood actor, Will Smith, were amongst the winners at the recently held 2022 Golden Globe Awards.

Despite being pulled by NBC following a controversy surrounding the voting body, the Hollywood Foreign Press Association, revealed the winners of the 79th Golden Globe at the Beverly Hilton Hotel in LA, California on Sunday.

The event awarded 2021 film and TV productions from 2021 in a scaled-down presentation due to the rise in COVID-19, according to Entertainment Weekly.
