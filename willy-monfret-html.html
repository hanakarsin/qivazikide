Willy Monfret - Age, Family, Bio

International model and DJ who was noticed by Nicki Minaj after he moved to New York. He played the lead role in Minaj's "Right Tru Me" music video and later released his own tracks called "Fux" and "Rise of Machines."

Before Fame

He ran for the French National Team before he became a model.

Trivia

He started modeling for Karin Models and has worked with brands including Ralph Lauren, Hugo Boss, Calvin Klein, Vogue, Benetton, Carrera & L'Oreal. He played the lead role in a short film called Love Bug which premiered at the Tribeca Screening Room.

Family Life

He was born in Paris, France. His work has taken him to Australia, Hong Kong and South Africa.

Associated With

He modeled for P. Diddy's Sean John clothing line.
