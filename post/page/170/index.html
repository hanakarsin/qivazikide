Scores Of Bandits Killed As Military Jets Bombarded Camps In Katsina And Sokoto

published on May 04, 2024
Troops of the Nigerian Military have neutralised several bandit and destroyed their camps in Sokoto and Katsina forests. It was gathered that Nigerian Airforce attached to Operation Hadarin Daji bombarded bandits camps in Mashema, Yanfako, Gebe, and Gatawa Forests all located in Isaa and Sabon Birni local government areas of Sokoto State. According to military sources, the strikes were executed on October 5, 2021. “We observed that structures and logistics items camouflaged under the dense vegetation, were being used as staging areas where some bandits from Turji and Maigona Camps and their fighters meet to plan and launch attacks,” a source told PRNigeria.
