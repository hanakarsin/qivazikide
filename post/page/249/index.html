Nnamdi Kanu Is No Longer IPOB's Leader

Simon Ekpa a self-acclaimed follower of the Indigenous People of Biafra, IPOB, leader, Nnamdi Kanu has said that the agitator no longer leads the group. He said that the activist has been removed as the leader of the group and has been replaced with Sofia Chinyere Kalu.
