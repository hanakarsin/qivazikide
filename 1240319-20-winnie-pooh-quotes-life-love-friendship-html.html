20 best Winnie the Pooh quotes about life, love and friendship

The story of the resilient bear and his best friend Piglet became popular immediately after its creation. Probably everyone has heard of Winnie the Pooh, and given the bear's wisdom and the entertainment offered by the cartoon, it is no wonder why. There are many deep, funny and interesting Winnie the Pooh quotes that you should learn.

Best Winnie the Pooh quotes

The image of this charming bear is the brainchild of Alan Alexander Milne, inspired by the cute games of his son and his plush friends. Although the Pooh is sometimes viewed as a sluggish and overly quiet character, his unpretentious naturalness is a good example of a wise outlook on life, not losing relevance over the years. The following Winnie the Pooh quotes will help you to awaken your inner child.

  • “I think we dream so we don’t have to be apart so long. If we’re in each other’s dreams we can be together all the time.”
  • “You find sometimes that a thing which seemed very thingish inside you is quite different when it gets out into the open and has other people looking at it.”
  • “If you live to be a hundred, I want to live to be a hundred minus one day so I never have to live without you.”
  • “It is more fun to talk with someone who doesn’t use long, difficult words but rather short, easy words like, “What about lunch?””
