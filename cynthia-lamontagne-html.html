Cynthia LaMontagne - Age, Family, Bio

Most well known for her portrayal of Rhonda in the popular television series That '70s Show, she is also recognized for her appearances in episodes of Ellen, ER, Dharma & Greg, and Criminal Minds.

Before Fame

She first appeared on screen in a bit part in the 1993 film Carlito's Way.

Trivia

She played the same character, Annie, in two episodes of the sitcom Frasier: "The Ski Lodge" (1998) and "Don Juan in Hell: Part II" (2001).

Family Life

She married Aaron Zelman in 2006. Through her marriage, she is the sister-in-law of producer and screenwriter Daniel Zelman.

Associated With

She appeared with Jennifer Grant in the 1998 comedy movie My Engagement Party.
