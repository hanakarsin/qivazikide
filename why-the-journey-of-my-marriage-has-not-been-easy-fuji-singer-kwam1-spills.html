Why The Journey Of My Marriage Has Not Been Easy

Nigerian fuji singer, Wasiu Ayinde Marshall, popularly known as Kwam1, has disclosed one of the major challenges in his marriage as he celebrates his first wedding anniversary with his wife, Emmanuella Ropo.

Naija News reports that Kwam1 got married to Emmanuella in November 2021, at a star-studded event, and after the marriage, there were rumours of the singer cheating on his wife.

Taking to his Instagram page on Friday, Kwam1 shared an adorable photo of his wife as he eulogized her, saying she is an angel sent from God and he would adore and cherish her for the rest of his life.

According to him, the journey of their marriage has not been easy due to side talks, notwithstanding, God has helped them weather the storm.

He wrote: “A year ago, I announce the beginning of a new chapter in my love life, with the arrival of my Angel sent from heavenly father, to love, adore and cherish for the rest of my life. Ajike Miiii, the journey isn’t that easy because of side talks, but we weather the storm by the special grace of God Almighty.

“May we continue to enjoy the blessings as dictate by God the more. Happy Wedding Anniversary to us AYAMII.. Itesi Oroki Omo Olukojiya, same you Ojanimogun ara ijamo, Omo Epo N pon roro. Aya Omoba Fidipotemole Ojusagbola Ogoji @emmanuellaeversmiling.”
