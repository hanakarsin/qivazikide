E-bill – how to check KPLC token balance

E-bill is an electronic email linked method used to check kplc prepaid token balance. It is pretty simple and gives you all information regarding your electricity account and is also used to make queries. It is super effective with almost impromptu responses to their clients. The basic steps to have you token balance details are as follows:

  • Get online and log in to your mailbox
  • Click on the compose prompt to get open a new E-mail
  • Type the account number (the first part) in the subject field.
  • Send the blank mail to kplc.co.ke
  • An automatic response comes into your mail with all information including balance and payment due date. Should this response fail to be received immediately, then you need to check if you correctly entered the account number if connectivity is a non-issue here.

How to check KPLC token balance using KenyaPowerBot

This is yet another upscale method of check KPLC token balance. It work undeniably well and is highly reliable and accurate too. Telegram Messenger, a messaging app has collaborated with Kenya Power & Lightning Company to make how to check KPLC token balance amazingly simple. Thanks to KenyaPowerBot you can get a history about your power account as opposed to only getting the current state only. To successful get you account information follow this step by step procedure;

  • Have the telegram messenger app installed on your phone or electronic device.
  • Use @KenyaPowerBot search engine and follow the bot.
  • This directs you to KenyaPowerBot homepage; presenting you with diverse options of either postpaid bill, monthly charge, last token, last 5 tokens and debt. Execute the option you are interested in.
  • Enter your 11 meter number and send. Account information then displays if you did enter the account number correctly. The boon is that the bot keeps memory of these numbers so you don’t have to key in every time you checking the token balance.
