Copy and Paste: Hilarious reactions to photos of Ferdinand Waititu's son after they went viral

- Photos and a video of Ferdinand Waititu's son, Brian Ndung'u, in court went viral on social media

- The young man was arrested on a charge of driving under the influence

- Many Kenyans found huge similarities between the father and son, from their appearance to some habits

Kenyans have hilariously reacted to Ferdinand Waititu's son's photos, which went viral on Twitter after his recent arrest.

Brian Ndug'u Waititu was arrested on charges of driving under the influence and appeared in court on Monday, March 16, to answer his case.

Photos of him at the dock went viral on social media, with many sharing their views on the politician's last born son.

Many were initially surprised that the son of a filthy rich politician was apprehended while driving a Toyota Probox but quickly remembered that his father would have done the same.

Appearing before Nairobi Senior Principal Magistrate Esther Kimilu, Brian pleaded guilty and was slapped with a three-month jail term or pay KSh 30,000 cash bail.

However, the Strathmore University law student could not raise the money and was later transferred to the Industrial Area Remand to serve his sentence.

A source intimated to TUKO.co.ke that the learner tried to contact his father to pay the cash bail, but he dismissed him, saying he should be jailed to experience prison life.

"Wacha akae huko ndani askie vile kuko, (let them jail him so that he can experience the prison life)," said Waititu.

Ndung'u also contacted his mother, who also shared the same sentiments.

While Speaking to TUKO.co.ke, Ndung'u said he was ready to serve the three-month jail term.

"I have been arrested more than 15 times so going to jail for me is not a big deal," he said.
