Top 15 online coding courses with certificates to enroll in 2021

Online coding courses have become essential in the modern-day technological world. You can use them to get started with programming, become a novice coder, or develop your skillset. But the challenge you will face is that there are many online coding courses with certificates to choose from, some of which offer high-quality education, and others are below average.

Nowadays, some online coding courses have curriculums. These curriculums help new learners to plan their programming lessons and get references that ensure they are on the right track.

They also offer components designed to help new coders to develop concepts, from a foundational level to increasingly complex skills or topics. However, these programmes are available online in plenty that choosing the best can be daunting.

15 best online coding courses with certificates

What is the best online coding course with a certificate to enrol? With thousands of courses available, here are 15 of them that are worth considering.

1. Code.org

Code.org is an online platform where students expand their knowledge of computer science. It has hundreds of mini-games and tutorials designed to get learners interested in coding. The website offers the best coding courses for beginners backed by a curriculum like lessons plans, unplugged activities, and videos.

The content on Code.org helps to teach students concepts that they can reinforce through various programming stages. Through the website, kids can learn computer science, make games, and develop apps with ease. Code.org offers free curriculum and open source technology. Learners get a certificate upon completing their studies.

2. Codecademy

Codecademy is one of the largest providers of online coding instructions. The platform has been offering programming lessons for seven years and has educated over 45 million learners. Unlike many similar services, the site allows individuals to choose what to learn. You can choose from building websites to analysing data or learn programming and computer science.

All lessons on Codecademy are free. However, premium programmes are available that allow tailored tutorials. You also get a certificate upon completion.

3. Coursera

Coursera is one of the best places to learn to code online. The platform partners with top universities and technology experts to offer students high-quality courses. Its programmes are available in flexible schedules that learners can accommodate.

Some of the best Coursera coding classes to enrol include:

  • Computer Science: Programming with a Purpose
  • SQL for Data Science
  • Programming Foundations with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS
  • Python for Everybody
  • Machine Learning

Coursera has many courses that you can access free. However, many of the programmes have certificates that you will have to pay for upon completion of your studies.

4. DataCamp

DataCamp is a website that allows learners to get the skills they need in the world of technology. It also helps experts to expand their knowledge on data and get the training that will allow them to solve real-world problems. Through DataCamp, you can track your progress after finishing a course and practice with quick daily challenges.

DataCamp offers coding certification online. You will get a certificate once you complete a programming course like R or Python. The programmes are available for free and at a premium rate.

5. Edureka

Edureka is an online training provider that offers certification courses and master programmes. Novices and advanced coders can learn trending technologies and get certified in some of the most demanded courses by the world’s top companies. Through the platform, you can master top tech skills like:

  • DevOps
  • Robotics Process Automation
  • Python Programming
  • UiPath
  • Bi and Visualisation

edX is one of the most trusted platforms for learning and education. It is home to more than 20 million learners, who access over 2,000 free online courses from 140 leading institutions worldwide. freeCodeCamp is one of the best online platforms that offer free coding for beginners. Since the website started offering lessons via the internet, over 40,000 students have graduated from it. The site has a well-structured curriculum that will take your coding knowledge from a novice to an expert level.

8. GitHub

GitHub is a one-stop platform for everything you need about coding. You can use it as your tutorial guide to learn almost any programming language you want. It provides you with step-by-step documentation of any framework, project, or coding language.

If you are looking for online medical coding courses, you can use GitHub to learn more about medical coding. The site has content on how to apply machine learning methods to medical images. Also, it gives you access to an open-source code base for enabling software innovation in medical imaging. Everything provided on the platform is free.

9. Kaggle

Kaggle is one of the leading platforms for learning more about data science. It is an online community that offers advanced tools and resources that help individuals to expand their knowledge and skills of data science. The website prepares complex topics to their fundamental components to make it easy for learners to understand them.

12. Scratch

Scratch is one of the best online coding courses for kids, beginners, and even experts. It allows children to programme their own interactive stories, animation, and games.
