King Yella - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper known for tracks such as "Ready Set Go" and "How I Do It." He was featured on Vlad TV in 2015 where he discussed the Chicago rap scene. He occasionally collaborates with other artsits.

Before Fame

He released his first single, "Swagg Check," on August 6, 2010. 

Trivia

He was featured on the hip hop website WorldStarHipHop. He launched a GoFundMe campaign in November 2015 to help support Chicago's youth. He has more than 6,000 monthly listeners on Spotify.

Family Life

He is part of the Skeezegang.

Associated With

He is a contemporary of fellow Chicago-based rapper Chance The Rapper.
