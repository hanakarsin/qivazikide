President Buhari Returns To Abuja From Imo

published on April 15, 2024
President Muhammadu Buhari has returned to Abuja after embarking on a one-day working visit to Imo State. Naija News reports that the Presidential jet landed at the private wing of the Nnamdi Azikwe International Airport around 05:00 pm. Thereafter, Buhari’s chopper arrived at the Villa premises at about 05:32 pm. Earlier, the President had inaugurated several infrastructural projects completed by the Hope Uzodinma administration. Some of the projects commissioned are the newly reconstructed Imo State House of Assembly Complex, the 36-kilometre dualized Owerri/Orlu road and the first phase of the dualised 53-kilometre Owerri/Okigwe Road.
