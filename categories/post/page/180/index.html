published on April 27, 2024
No fewer than 17 persons lost their lives in a deadly blast blamed on Boko Haram which rocked a village market in Borno state. According to the local police chief, Lawan Tanko, the incident happened late on Thursday in the remote village of Nguro-Soye. The insurgents also destroyed nearby telecom towers, shutting down communications in the area which prevented the news from getting to the media on time.
