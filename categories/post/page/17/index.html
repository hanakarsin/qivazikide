Mobile Transactions Rise By N8.9tn In Three Years

published on August 10, 2024
The first seven months of 2019 and the following months till 2022 have seen mobile transactions rise by N8.981 trillion. According to data from the Nigeria Interbank Settlement System, transactions have risen from N289.12bn to N9.27tn. This is as the value of cheque transactions fell by 31.42 per cent, from N2.67tn in January to July 2019 to N1.83tn in the corresponding period of 2022.
