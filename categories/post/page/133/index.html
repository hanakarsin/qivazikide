Naija News reports that the composition of the committee was announced in a statement on Friday by the National Publicity Secretary of the party, Kola Ologbondiyan. The committee is tasked with collating inputs from stakeholders, as well as engage relevant civil society groups and work closely with the National Assembly for more inclusive and accelerated processes in the “desired amendment” to the act and the constitution.
