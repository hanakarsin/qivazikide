Amnesty Programme: Bandit Attacks Reducing In Katsina State

published on March 23, 2024
The Governor of Katsina State, Aminu Masari, has revealed that the amnesty programme created for repentant bandits and other strategies is paying off following the reduction in the spate of attacks by bandits in the state. He further stated that eight local government area bordering the Rugu forest still experience bandits attacks despite the efforts of the state government. published on March 23, 2024
The presidential candidate of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP), former Vice President Atiku Abubakar has confirmed an attack on his Yola residence by suspected member of Boko Haram. Naija News earlier reported that former presidential aide, Reno Omokri, claimed that a terrorist invaded Atiku’s residence on his Twitter handle on Monday.
