Man Who Broke up With Lover 2 Years after Engagement Finally Marries Her

- Bugoma, a music director and his wife Florence, a church choir leader, have an amazing love story

- The couple broke up after two years of dating and later rekindled their love

- Their wedding was attended by 200 guests due to the COVID-19 restrictions

The Bible says who finds a woman finds a beautiful thing. The love story of Ugandan couple Julius Ssengooba, aka JJ Bugoma, and Florence Nambasa is beautiful.

Bugoma, a music director and his wife Florence, a church choir leader, met a few years ago and fell in love with each other.

They started dating, but two years later, things were not working, and they decided to part ways.

"At one point, the vibe was no more, the excitement was over and I felt nothing was new. But we later mended fences and moved on,” Bugoma told My Wedding.

The two later rekindled their love, and on August 18, 2020, Bugoma decided to propose to the lover of his life.

He went to a Century Cinemax, where Florence was watching a film

"We tricked Florence into what we called ‘a love scandal’ that involved our mutual friends and she was meant to meet her friends to arbitrate the matter and she fell for it," he said.

Florence arrived at the cinema to watch a film of her choice but was halted midday because of a glitch.

Bugoma took the chance to propose to his sweetheart, and she said yes.

"I called her name and maybe she thought it was in the film. I raised my voice and the lights went on. She slapped me and I thought I was all a mess but she said yes," he said.

Bugoma ad Florence went ahead and held their traditional wedding and later a white wedding.

Their friends and family contributed to their wedding, which 200 guests attended due to the Covid-19 restrictions.
