Douglas Emhoff - Age, Family, Bio

Lawyer and entertainment litigator who is married to U.S. Vice President Kamala Harris. He opened his own firm in 2000 and six years later it was acquired by Venable LLP. He then became the managing director of Venable's West Coast offices.

Before Fame

He has attended the University of Southern California.

Trivia

In 2021, he became the first male spouse of a Vice President in U.S. history. 

Family Life

He has two children with his first wife, Kerstin.

Associated With

His wife became Vice President alongside President Joe Biden.
