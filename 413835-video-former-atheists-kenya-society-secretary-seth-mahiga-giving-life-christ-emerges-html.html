Mahiga's resignation

Mahiga stepped down from the position, citing he had found Jesus Christ and is no longer interested in promoting atheism in Kenya.

In a statement on Saturday, May 29, the society's president Harrison Mumia eulogised him as a dedicated leader, wishing him the best in his newfound relationship with Jesus Christ.

"We wish Seth all the best in his newfound relationship with Jesus Christ, we thank him for having served the society with dedication over the last one and half years," read the statement in part.
