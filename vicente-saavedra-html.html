Vicente Saavedra - Age, Family, Bio

Entrepreneur and talent agent known throughout the Latin music industry for helping to launch the career of superstar Ozuna. He has more than 180,000 followers on his verified Instagram account.

Before Fame

Early in his career he worked for the independent record labels White Lion Records and Buddha's Family.

Trivia

He committed himself to the development of Ozuna's career in 2015. Two years later, he was named one of Billboard Magazine's 40 Under 40.
