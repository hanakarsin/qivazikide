Kylie Jenner Gets Dumped

Kylie Jenner loses a member of his entourage. The youngest billionaire in the world has been dumped, Us Weekly reports .

Kylie Jenner had to say goodbye to her personal assistant Victoria Villarroel, who decided to leave her in order to pursue a career on social networks!

“Kylie and Victoria are still friends and close. Kylie has no bad feelings against Victoria and supports her, “said a source who is said to be close to the situation.

Jenner hired Villarroel a while ago to take care of some of her personal affairs.

Remember that Kylie Jenner had to thank another assistant in the past, who would have revealed personal information about the Kardashian-Jenner clan in an interview!
