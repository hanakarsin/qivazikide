Skilah Hurd - Age, Family, Bio

Digital creator known for her skillsawww TikTok account, which she uses for dance, comedy, and trends. She has accumulated 1.4 million fans. She often uses text to discuss relatable romantic problems and situations. 

Before Fame

She started singing as a child. She became active on TikTok in October of 2021.   

Trivia

She is also a member of Fanfix. Singing and videography are passions of hers. She became a student at Grand Canyon University in Arizona. 

Family Life

She was born in the United States. 

Associated With

In October of 2021, she posted a comedic TikTok about dating and set it to "Careless Whisper" by George Michael. She featured fellow social influencer Sadie Mckenna in an Instagram post in June 2022 with pictures taken on Hollywood Blvd.
