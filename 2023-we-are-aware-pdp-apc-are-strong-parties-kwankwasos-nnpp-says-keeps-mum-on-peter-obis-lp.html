We Are Aware PDP, APC Are Strong Parties Kwankwasos NNPP Says, Keeps Mum On Peter Obi's LP

The National Chairman of the Party, Prof. Rufai Alkali, disclosed this while speaking with newsmen on Wednesday in Lagos.

Alkali insisted that the kind of leadership the NNPP would be bringing is totally different from what others are offering or have been offering.

He argued that the NNPP is waxing stronger daily and are getting ready for the campaign.

While he acknowledged the strength of the APC and PDP, he, however, kept mum on Peter Obi’s Labour Party.

He said, “We’re out to reduce the drift and sharp edges of anger and bitterness in the country.

“NNPP is a party that is rooted in a national movement that will usher in a greater Nigeria.

“We have now got our candidates at different levels, and we’re getting ready for the campaigning time soon for the elections.

“Of course, we’re not oblivious of the power of what PDP and APC can do.

“Our party is on course. We’re very strong. We are waxing stronger. We are concerned about the future of Nigeria and our democracy.”
