How to respond to ‘Hey’ from a girl

Are you wondering what to say when a girl texts you 'Hey'? Traditionally, men are expected to initiate a conversation. Unfortunately, the cultural habit has persisted into the online dating universe.

Therefore, getting the first message from a girl sounds strange for most people, as the woman sets the initial tone and expectation for a conversation.

However, if you are unfortunate to get the generic, 'Hey,' you might wrack your brain trying to figure out the ideal way to respond. So, why is it hard to respond to such a message?

First, the three-letter word is vague as it doesn't give one the flow they ought to expect from a conversation. Second, it is the texting equivalent to a nod of the head. The person is opening the door for you to chat, but you are responsible for continuing the conversation.

Give them a taste of their own medicine

One of the best responses to the 'Hey' text is responding with 'Hey' back. You can even tweak the response to include more words and have something like, 'Hey, how are you.'

However, keep in mind that 'Hey' is not a great conversation starter. It would not come out well when you respond to a stranger with the three-letter word, especially on a dating app. And it is even worse if it is your crush, as you will sound non-interested in pursuing them. Sometimes, 'Hey' can be interpreted as rude.

If you find 'Hey' an ice breaker, as most people do, sometimes it is a great idea to be direct and say like it is. You can ask the lady to spice things up. Of course, the reply wouldn't go well with some people. Therefore, it is advisable to use such responses in conversations that you are less concerned about as you do not want to lose your crush's attention.

Another interesting way of how to respond to ‘Hey’ from a girl is to convert the conversation into a fun, creative story. Such a story would get the other individual involved in the discussion, making it even more enjoyable.

Do a clickbait

A clickbait is a great way to make someone intrigued and want to find out more. And you can create the perfect clickbait with three to four words, perfectly designed to make her crave an answer. For example, you can use words like:

  • Hey (name), do you know what makes people attracted to you?
  • Hey (name), do you know what makes you interesting?

You can also convert the clickbait to a joke by asking something that will require a funny response. Making a joke allows you to test their sense of humour, which can help you decide if you are compatible.

However, ascertain that you tweak your clickbait well to elicit a funny reaction. Some jokes can be offensive, and it is a quick way of making someone lose interest.

Have you ever thought of using sarcasm in your first conversation with a stranger? Many people looking for more information on how to reply to a 'Hey' text have tried it, and it has worked amazingly well.
