Ryan Bergara - Age, Family, Bio

Video producer at BuzzFeed who became the creator and host of BuzzFeed Unsolved. He's also done work as a director, cinematographer, writer and editor. He became a co-founder and host of production studio Watcher.

Before Fame

He attended Chapman University where he studied narrative television. He had an internship with Sony Pictures and Viacom.

Trivia

He first began working with BuzzFeed in 2014 as a video intern. He was then a video fellow before reaching producer status. He posted a video of himself proposing to his girlfriend on his Instagram page and the video has received over 180 thousand hearts.

Family Life

He began a relationship with Helen Pan. He later became engaged to Marielle Scott.

Associated With

He and Shane Madej host Buzzfeed Unsolved on YouTube.
