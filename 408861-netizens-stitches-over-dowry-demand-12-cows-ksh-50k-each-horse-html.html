Netizens in Stitches Over Dowry Demand of '12 Cows for KSh 50k Each and Horse'

- A South African social media user has shared how her cousin was asked what sounded like an unrealistic lobola (bride price)

- Going by the name of @ChinaLikuye on social media, the lady said the exorbitant lobola (dowry) demands left her laughing

- The social networking community in South Africa was in stitches as some questioned what went wrong with the old dowry tradition

As a sign of commitment, South African men are expected to pay lobola for their women, but a local social media user is shocked at how much her cousin was 'charged'.

According to @ChinaLikuye, her cousin decided to show his affection for his lover and opted to take the next step, which is paying lobola (dowry) towards getting married.

However, the social media user says her cousin was asked for an exorbitant price, coupled with live animals.
