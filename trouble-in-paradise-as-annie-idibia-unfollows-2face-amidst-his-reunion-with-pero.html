Trouble In Paradise As Annie Idibia Unfollows 2face Amidst His Reunion With Pero And Her Children In

There seems to be an issue between Nollywood actress Annie Idibia and her husband, 2face Idibia as she has unfollowed him on Instagram.

This comes barely a few weeks after 2face’s baby mama Pero Adeniyi took to his Instagram page to shower love on the singer for being a doting father and spending quality time with her daughter during a school game in the US.

Sharing a photo of the duo, Pero Adeniyi described 2face as her daughter’s number one cheerleader and loudest track dad.

2face also took to his Instagram story to share adorable moments with his three kids whom he had with Pero Adeniyi.

In the series of posts, 2face was seen beaming with smiles and vibing with his children.

However, it seemed this didn’t go well with Annie Idibia as she unfollowed her husband on the photo and video sharing app, Instagram.

Although, 2face is still following Annie Idibia and it is uncertain if the singer and his wife are having issues.

Often times when a couple unfollows each other on social media, it is always assumed that they might be having some issue in their marriage.
