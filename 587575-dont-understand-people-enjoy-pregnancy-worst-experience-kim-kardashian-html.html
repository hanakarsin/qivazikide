I Dont Understand People Who Enjoy Pregnancy, It Is My Worst Experience Kim Kardashian

Kim Kardashian isn’t mincing words as regards her pregnancy and all she has had to deal with in the wake of it. She says it is the worst experience of her life.

[article_adwert]

The 34-year-old is pregnant with her second child with rapper and fashionista Kanye West, with a delivery slated for December 25, according to inside sources.

The TV star took to her website and shared her experience so far with her second pregnancy. From all indications it’s been a challenging period for the reality star.

“I’m gonna keep it real: For me, pregnancy is the worst experience of my life! LOL,” she wrote on KimKardashianWest.com.

She explained how much she doesn’t fancy the experience of being pregnant even though others might.

She wrote further: “I don’t enjoy one moment of it and I don’t understand people who enjoy it. My mom and Kourtney clearly did! More power to them but I just don’t relate!”

Mrs. West is uncomfortable with her pregnancy experience but she doesn’t give any particular reasons behind her reactions.

“I’m not sure why I don’t like the experience like others do. Maybe it’s the swelling, the backaches or just the complete mindf*** of how your body expands and nothing fits...I just always feel like I’m not in my own skin. It’s hard to explain. I don’t feel sexy, either—I feel insecure and most of the time I just feel gross,” she wrote.
