Conor Jack McGregor - Age, Family, Bio

Widely known as the first-born child of MMA superstar Conor McGregor and his long-time girlfriend Dee Devlin. His parents had been together for nine years prior to his May 2017 birth. 

Before Fame

His father rose to international acclaim for his torrid run through the UFC's featherweight and lightweight divisions, which began in 2008. 

Trivia

His birth was covered by various news magazines and tabloids, including Fox Sports, People Magazine, and CBS Sports. 

Family Life

He has two aunts named Aoife and Erin. 

Associated With

His father, Conor McGregor, became the first fighter to hold championships in two weight divisions simultaneously after winning the lightweight championship at UFC 205.
