Jay Boogie Blasts Bobrisky Over Social Media Feud

Nigerian cross-dresser Daniel Anthony, popularly known as Jay Boogie has berated controversial cross-dresser, Eedris Okuneye, Bobrisky.

This is following the male barbie’s clash with former bestie Tonto Dikeh and businessman, Mompha on social media.

Reacting to the development, Jay Boogie who identifies as a transwoman slammed Bobrisky, whilst questioning why he enjoys fighting people on social media.

According to Jay, Bobrisky should ”have sense and stop painting the trans community bad.”

He also slammed Bobrisky for dragging Mompha who he described as an “innocent married man” into his “public display of shenanigans”.

Jay Boogie wrote; “Queens would always be queens. Idris, it’s obvious you don’t even comprehend the basics of life and how to appreciate the good things you once had and have lost. We keep indulging you to have sense and stop mortifying the trans community as often as you do. I’m irked at your mischievous antiques and the zeal you exude when you exhibit your psychosis.

“How can you be so incorrigible and reprehensible? No self-integrity, no shame, no dignity and no prestige! Nne it is appalling. What could be so demeaning? What did tontolet ever do to you to deserve such disrespect and hate. It’s just exasperating that the public sees the rest of us through you. How on earth can be ever gain respect and stand with our heads up high with your constant display of stupidity and foolishness.

“Even an innocent married man mompha you had to drag him in your public display of shenanigans. Your enthusiasm and resilience towards squabbles and altercations baffles me. You’re pushing 30 and you’re this infantile?”
