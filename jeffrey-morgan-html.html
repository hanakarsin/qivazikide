Jeffrey Dean Morgan - Age, Family, Bio

Gained wide recognition for his roles on the television series The Walking Dead, Supernatural, and Grey's Anatomy. He also played the role of The Comedian in the 2009 film Watchmen. 

Before Fame

He was a basketball player in high school and college and was later employed as a graphic artist. 

Trivia

He played Judah Botwin on the television series Weeds and he portrayed Jason Crouse on The Good Wife from 2015 to 2016. 

Family Life

He was married to actress Anya Longwell from 1992 to 2003. He married actress Hilarie Burton in 2019. They have a son named Gus and a daughter named George together. 

Associated With

He was briefly engaged to his Weeds co-star Mary-Louise Parker in 2008.
