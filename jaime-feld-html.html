Jaime Feld - Age, Family, Bio

Wife of former Gossip Girl TV star Kevin Zegers. The two married on August 3, 2013 in New Jersey after having dated for years prior.

Before Fame

After graduating college, she worked as a teacher for junior high aged kids with disabilities. She moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in the entertainment industry, and began working as the assistant to the owner of the CAA talent agency.

Trivia

She has worked as a talent agent for various high profile actors, actresses, and musicians at Creative Artist's Agency (CAA). They include Sofia Vergara and Miley Cyrus.

Family Life

She gave birth to twin daughters, Zoe and Blake, in August 2015.

Associated With

She has also counted Chelsea Handler, who she considers a close friend, as one of her clients at CAA.
