KNEC CP2 portal: registration, deployment letters, reset password

KNEC (Kenya National Examinations Council) is obligated to establish and maintain exam standards in Kenya. Every year, the council hires qualified overseers, assessors, and supervisors to ascertain that national examinations are appropriately conducted. You must be registered through the KNEC CP2 portal and meet all other stated criteria to qualify for these roles.

TABLE OF CONTENTS

The Contracted Professionals System, commonly known as the KNEC CP2 portal, is an integrated online system that allows the council to manage all national examination officers. It captures accurate details of all people contracted to supervise, invigilate, and manage exams.

How to use the KNEC CP2 portal

The KNEC CP2 portal has enhanced the efficiency and accountability of the council. It integrates different agencies and service providers to gather, verify, and report on activities and personnel involved in issuing national exams. Once you register in the portal, you can apply for contract jobs whenever they are announced and download your deployment letter.

How do I register with KNEC CP2?

The KNEC CP2 registration process is straightforward. Contracted professionals must submit their details through the portal while applying for various positions, including national exam supervision, invigilation and management. If you are using the CP2 KNEC portal for the first time, please follow this registration process:

  • Ensure your computer is connected to the internet, then open the browser of your choice.
  • Visit the KNEC CP2 portal to create an account.
  • The interface that comes up is for logging in. Below the login tabs, you will see Create account or Reset Password options. Click on Create account.
  • Fill in your mobile phone number on the interface that pops up, confirm the number entered is correct, and click Create Account.
  • You will receive an SMS on your mobile phone giving you the login details, i.e. username and password. The message will also give you the link to access the system.
  • Once you receive the details, confirm receipt of the login credentials from the phone number used to create the account.
  • Before proceeding to the next step, it is crucial to download the manual provided on the portal to guide you in accessing all services offered.

How to log in to the KNEC CP2 portal?

Existing users require their usernames and passwords to access the portal. Follow this KNEC CP2 portal login guide:

  • Open the browser of your choice on an internet-connected device.
  • Visit the KNEC CP2 homepage that looks like the image below.
  • Enter your username and password to log in.

How to reset the CP2 KNEC account password

If you have forgotten the password to your account, send an SMS with the word Password to 22262. A response will be sent to your phone number to help you access your account.

Alternatively, you can visit the portal and click Forgot password under the login tab. You will be required to provide your username to reset it.

How to apply for jobs through the CP2 KNEC portal

You can apply for jobs after creating a CP2 KNEC portal login account. Vacancies are grouped by exam type, activity type, designation, duty station, county, and sub-county. To apply for a position, click on each dropdown tab and choose as guided by the table below.

How to withdraw an application on the KNEC CP2 portal

You can withdraw a successful application if you made an error like choosing the wrong designation or have changed your mind:

  • Log in to your CP2 KNEC portal account.
  • Go to the Applications tab and click withdraw.

NB: You can make a new application after the withdrawal.

How to download deployment letters

Successful applicants normally get deployment letters to show they have secured the contracted jobs. The letter is evidence that an invigilator or supervisor should be in a particular workstation. The deployment letters can be accessed through the portal using these:

  • Visit the KNEC CP2 portal and log in to your account.
  • Click the Dashboard tab.
  • Click the Deployment tab to show the centre and position you have secured.
  • Under the Deployment section, you will also see a table. Beneath the table is an Actions column, and under it is a Download option.
  • Click Download to access your deployment letter.

How do I register with KNEC?

You can register with the national examinations council by visiting the CP2 portal and creating an account. Ensure you remember your login details, as you will require them whenever you wish to access the portal.

How do I activate my CP2?

There is no special activation needed to use the CP2 portal. All you need to do is create an account on the portal following the steps explained above. Once you create the account, you can apply for vacant positions and download deployment letters if you successfully apply.

How do I register as an invigilator?

You can only apply for an invigilation position when KNEC opens the portal for applications. Log in to your account and choose the invigilator position under the activity dropdown (number 2 on the table).

The KNEC CP2 portal is an important platform for every person who wishes to invigilate or supervise national examinations in Kenya. Without registering on this portal, you cannot get these jobs.
