Ayame Misaki - Age, Family, Bio

Japanese actress who has gained international recognition for portraying Saori Shibuki in the Netflix science fiction thriller drama series Alice in Borderland. She has acted in a variety of Japanese television series and had a leading role in Cutie Honey: The Live. It was her performance in Cutie Honey: The Live that catapulted her to fame in the Japanese acting world.

Before Fame

She began her entertainment career as a gravure model and variety show participant.

Trivia

She won the Best Gravure Award at the 29th Horipro Talent Scout Caravan. She has appeared in advertisements for Sapporo beer.

Family Life

She has four sisters. Her dog Malone is often featured on her Instagram account.

Associated With

She worked alongside Kento Yamazaki on the cast of Alice in Borderland.
