Simon Lizotte - Age, Family, Bio

Professional disc golfer who is best known for having won six Elite Series titles. In January of 2023, he signed a ten-year sponsorship deal with MVP Discs that was reported to be worth more than $1M per year. He has also gained popularity for his self-titled YouTube channel Simon Lizotte, where he shares disc golf content. 

Before Fame

He began his career in the year 2008.    

Trivia

He has been sponsored by companies such as Discmania Discs. He is noted for holding the disc golf distance world record for throwing a distance of 1,030 feet.

Family Life

He was born in Bremen, Germany, but he also holds Canadian citizenship through his father. He is based in the city of Shrewsbury, Massachusetts in the United States. He and his wife Natalia Lizotte have a son named Emmett. 

Associated With

In May of 2021, he posted a photo of himself alongside Tedy Bruschi.
